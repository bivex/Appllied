*EMERGENCY AID TO INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS*
As part of the efforts of the Secretariat for Integral Development of the OAS to work hand-in-hand with
current and prospective beneficiaries to help mitigate the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic, the Rowe
Fund Program is offering a one-time, $2,000 scholarship per candidate, upon the approval of an
interest-free loan, to Latin American and Caribbean citizens, who were adversely affected financially
by the pandemic and are completing their studies in U.S. universities.
Criteria/eligibility
Scholarship applicants should simultaneously be applying for a Rowe Fund interest-free loan, of at least
5,000 (here) and meet the eligibility requirements (here). In addition, scholarship applicants mus
ubmit this form through the online loan application platform or via email to: rowefund@oas.org
The Rowe Fund Committee is currently accepting applications and will review completed applications
upon their receipt. The number of scholarships to be awarded is limited.
